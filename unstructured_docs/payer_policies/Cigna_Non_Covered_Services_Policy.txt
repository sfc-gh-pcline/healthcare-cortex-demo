Coverage Policy: Non-Covered Services, Exclusions, and Benefit Limitations
Policy Number: CIGNA-COV-2025-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

SUBJECT: Services Excluded from Coverage and Denial Code PR-204

1. OVERVIEW

This policy outlines services that are excluded from coverage under standard Cigna benefit plans. Claims for excluded services will be denied with reason code PR-204 (This service/equipment/drug is not covered under the patient's current benefit plan). Members are responsible for the full cost of non-covered services.

2. GENERAL EXCLUSIONS

The following services are NOT covered under standard benefit plans:

2.1 Experimental/Investigational Procedures:
   - Procedures not approved by the FDA for the specific indication
   - Off-label use of devices without peer-reviewed supporting evidence
   - Clinical trial procedures (unless specifically covered by the member's plan)
   - Gene therapy for conditions without FDA approval

2.2 Cosmetic Services:
   - Procedures performed primarily to improve appearance without documented functional impairment
   - Examples: rhinoplasty (cosmetic), blepharoplasty (cosmetic), abdominoplasty, liposuction
   - Exception: Reconstructive surgery following trauma, cancer treatment, or congenital anomaly IS covered

2.3 Out-of-Network Services (without authorization):
   - Services rendered by non-participating providers without prior authorization
   - Exception: Emergency services are covered regardless of network status
   - Exception: If no in-network provider is available within a reasonable distance

2.4 Duplicate Services:
   - Same service performed by the same provider on the same date of service
   - Same diagnostic test repeated without clinical justification
   - Claims submitted for services already paid under a different claim number

2.5 Services Exceeding Benefit Limits:
   - Physical therapy beyond the annual visit limit (typically 30 visits/year)
   - Mental health visits beyond the annual limit (if applicable)
   - DME exceeding the benefit period maximum

3. SPECIFIC NON-COVERED SERVICES

3.1 The following are specifically excluded:
   - Weight loss surgery for BMI < 35 (or < 30 without comorbidities)
   - Dental services (unless medical plan includes dental rider)
   - Vision correction surgery (LASIK, PRK) unless medically necessary
   - Hearing aids (unless plan includes hearing benefit)
   - Long-term custodial care
   - Personal comfort items during hospitalization
   - Travel or transportation (except approved ambulance transport)

4. COVERAGE EXCEPTION PROCESS

Members may request a coverage exception if they believe a denied service should be covered:

4.1 Standard Review: Submit written request with:
   - Letter from treating physician explaining medical necessity
   - Peer-reviewed clinical evidence supporting the service
   - Documentation of why alternative covered services are inadequate
   Timeline: Decision within 30 calendar days

4.2 Expedited Review: Available when standard timeline could jeopardize health
   Timeline: Decision within 72 hours

5. APPEAL PROCESS FOR PR-204 DENIALS

5.1 Internal Appeal:
   - Must be filed within 180 days of the denial notice
   - Include: denial letter, clinical records, physician letter, supporting evidence
   - Cigna will respond within 30 calendar days (standard) or 72 hours (expedited)

5.2 External Review:
   - Available after exhausting internal appeals
   - Must be requested within 4 months of final internal appeal denial
   - Conducted by an independent review organization (IRO)
   - IRO decision is binding on Cigna

6. MEMBER FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

When a service is denied as PR-204:
   - The member is responsible for the full billed amount
   - The service does not count toward deductible or out-of-pocket maximum
   - Providers must inform members of potential non-coverage BEFORE rendering services
   - An Advance Beneficiary Notice (ABN) or similar waiver should be signed
